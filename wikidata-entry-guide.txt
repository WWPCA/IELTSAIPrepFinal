WIKIDATA ENTRY GUIDE FOR IELTS AI PREP
Complete Instructions for Creating Your Knowledge Panel Entry
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: CREATE WIKIDATA ACCOUNT
→ Go to: https://www.wikidata.org
→ Click "Create account" (top right)
→ Choose a username and create your account
→ Verify your email

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 2: CREATE NEW ITEM
→ Once logged in, click "Create a new item" in the left sidebar
→ Or go directly to: https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Special:NewItem

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 3: FILL IN BASIC INFORMATION

LABEL (Required):
English: IELTS AI Prep

DESCRIPTION (Required):
English: AI-powered IELTS test preparation platform with instant band-aligned feedback for Writing and Speaking assessments

ALIASES (Optional but helpful):
- IELTS AI Preparation
- ieltsaiprep.com
- IELTS GenAI Prep

   Value: business (Q4830453)

   Value: https://www.ieltsaiprep.com

STEP 6: ADD REFERENCES

For each statement, click "add reference" and add:

Reference URL:
   Property: reference URL (P854)
   Value: https://www.ieltsaiprep.com

   Property: retrieved (P813)

STEP 7: REVIEW AND PUBLISH

→ Double-check all information is accurate
→ Click "Publish" at the bottom
→ Your Wikidata item is now live!

TIPS FOR SUCCESS:

1. Use accurate, verifiable information only
2. Keep descriptions neutral and factual
