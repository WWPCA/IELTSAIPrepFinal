Admin Password Reset

Your admin password is environment-secured for maximum security.

Since you are the system owner, you can reset your password by:

  1. Accessing your Replit dashboard
  2. Updating the ADMIN_PASSWORD_HASH secret
  3. The Lambda environment will automatically sync
Need help? Use the forgot password workflow on the regular login page, or update your secret in Replit.
