HOW TO CREATE YOUR LINKEDIN COMPANY PAGE:

1. Log into your personal LinkedIn account
2. Click the "Work" icon (grid of 9 dots) in the top right
3. Select "Create a Company Page"
4. Choose "Company" (not Showcase Page or Educational Institution)
5. Fill in the information below
